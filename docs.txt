As discussed, Please find the list of documents required to initiate Green Card processing.
1. PERM Questionnaire
2. Recent 3 months paystubs
3. All Academic certificates and transcripts (School to Masters)
4. Updated Resume
5. Passport (Except blank pages)
6. Form I-94 both sides
7. Birth/Marriage certificate
8. All I-20’s (Both Sides)
9. Copies of any experience letters from previous employers
10. EMPLOYMENT AUTHORIZATION DOCUMENT (EAD card)
11. NOTICE OF APPROVAL (H1B copy)
12. Previous I-94 forms (Both sides)
13. Latest W2
14. Cheque of $1500
